How to Use Expirer

This is a simple guide on how to securely upload and share PDFs.

  • Step 1: Click or drag into the upload box to select your PDF file.
  • Step 2: Enter the recipient's email address.
  • Step 3: Set an expiration date (month, day, and year) and time for the file.
  • Step 4: Click send to securely share the PDF.
  • Step 5: Copy the unique link and share it as needed.
